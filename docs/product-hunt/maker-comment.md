## Paste-ready comment

Hi Product Hunt — I built Founder OS because running a company of one often
means carrying goals, cash, pipeline, delivery, and every unfinished commitment
in the same head.

The first version exposed the machinery: thirteen specialist roles and dozens
of workflows. This release starts with the result instead. One resumable setup
turns what you already know about the business into one source-linked daily
decision, saved in local Markdown.

Who it is for: solo founders and small operators who want a working operating
rhythm without handing an autonomous agent their inbox, bank account, or CRM.
Founder OS never sends or pays. Each file has one explicit owner, every outside
claim keeps its source and date, and the founder still presses every real-world
button.

It is free, MIT-licensed, and runs as a Claude Code plugin. I would value the
most concrete feedback: try the activation flow and tell me where it breaks,
which question slows you down, or whether the first brief names something you
would genuinely do today.
