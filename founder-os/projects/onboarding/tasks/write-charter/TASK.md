Run `founder-os-init`. Scaffolds the workspace, sets your timezone on the
scheduled cadences, and interviews you for `charter.md`: who you are, what the
business is, what "won" looks like.

It starts on the first message. You are not asked whether now is a good time —
that question has one useful answer and asking it hands you somewhere else to be.

Do this first. Every other agent reads `charter.md` before it opens its mouth.

Twenty minutes, capped. Three steps after this one, and then the company hands
you your first daily brief — built from what you said in those twenty minutes.
