Run `icp-definition`. Who you serve, stated precisely enough that it excludes
people.

An ICP that excludes nobody is a mailing list. Expect this step to be
uncomfortable — that discomfort is the work.

You already handed `founder-os-init` the raw material: two clients you would
take again, one you would not, and what is different about them. This step
writes the ICP out of those three, now, in this session, and shows you the
result. It does not send you away to think about your positioning — a founder
who could state their ICP in the abstract would not have needed the examples.

If the ICP turns out to need something only you can get — a call with a past
client, a number off an old invoice — that is work, and work gets an id. Hand it
to the **Chief of Staff** for `queue.md`. No step here ends by telling you to go
and do something.
