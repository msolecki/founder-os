# Raised the strategy sprint floor to $15,000

Date: 2026-07-18

Decided by: founder

Serves: B1

## Context

June's fixed-scope sprint cleared the $200/h target while custom advisory fell
below it after 14 unbilled hours (metrics.md close dated 2026-07-01). The offer
needs to make the bounded work the default rather than the exception.

## Rejected

Keep a $10,000 entry sprint and upsell advisory later. It lost because the lower
price preserved the same interview and workshop load while attracting buyers
who wanted custom delivery.

## What would change our mind

Fewer than 2 qualified proposals at the $15,000 floor by 2026-08-31, provided at
least 8 ICP-matched discovery calls happened before that date.

## Supersedes

None.
