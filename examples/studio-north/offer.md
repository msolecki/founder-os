# Offer

## ICP

Founder-led B2B software companies with a working product, 10–50 employees, and
a sales team that describes the offer differently on every call.

### Not this

- pre-product startups looking for category discovery;
- companies asking for ongoing fractional marketing leadership;
- teams that cannot give the founder access to buyers and recorded calls.

## Offer

Three-week positioning sprint ending in a tested message, sales narrative, and
90-day rollout plan.

Boundary: strategy and enablement only. No website build, campaign operations,
or open-ended advisory.

## Pricing

$15,000 fixed fee. Fifty percent to book; fifty percent on delivery.
